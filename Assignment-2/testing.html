TESTING & Calibration

1.Power on the device & check if it powers up.

2.Check sensor reading in the Serial Monitor.

3.Verify that LED indicators are working based on the air quality.

4.Test the fan operation, both through automatic air quality control & manual button control.

5.Test filter effectiveness by observing the air quality improvement.

6.For ESP32,test Wi-Fi connection & remote control features.

7.Perform safety checks for overheating & proper protection.

8.Run the device for an extended period to test long-term functionally.

-Calibration : To calibrate the sensor, expose it to a known air quality level for 30 seconds".
